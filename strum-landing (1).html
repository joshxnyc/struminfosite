How It Works

Three steps to
any song.

Find your song
Search over a million songs, paste a YouTube link, upload your own audio, record live, or use Shazam to identify a song playing near you.

Features

Everything a guitarist
actually needs.

Not a lesson app. Not a tab site. Strum bridges the gap between the music you hear and the music you play — with full control at every step.

⚡
Smart Chord Recognition
Advanced AI analyzes audio waveforms to identify chord progressions with the accuracy of a trained musician — not keyword lookup. Every change, every voicing, every passing chord.
AI-Powered
♩
Strumming Patterns
Visual down/up stroke guides with BPM control and audio playback. No notation required — just follow the arrows.
✏️
Edit Any Chord
Tap any chord in the chart to replace it. Search by name, browse fingering diagrams, choose your voicing, and save — full control over every bar.
Full Control
🎸
Smart Capo Settings
Strum recommends the optimal capo position and retransposes every chord automatically. Adjust with ± and preview the full chord map before saving.
👥
Community Versions
Every song has multiple chord arrangements contributed by real players — different chords, different capo positions, different takes on the same song.
Community
